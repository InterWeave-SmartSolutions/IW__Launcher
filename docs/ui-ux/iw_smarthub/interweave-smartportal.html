How SmartPortal works

From customer login to payment recorded.

The SmartPortal manages the entire customer payment journey — from secure login to transaction posting — without any manual intervention from your team.

01
Register & Authenticate
Customers register and authenticate through the SmartPortal using the same permissions hierarchy as your CRM administration setup — no separate credential management.
→
02
Review Outstanding Invoices
Customers see their outstanding invoices, due dates, and account balance — pulled directly from your CRM in real time. Complete payment history and status for every invoice.
→
03
Make Payment
Customers pay via ACH, credit card, debit card, or mobile wallet — processed against your Merchant Service Provider. Any amount, any time, 24 hours a day.
→
04
Automatic CRM & Financial Update
Transaction Records are created in your CRM. These Sales Receipts are then transferred to your Financial Application — QuickBooks, Sage, MS Dynamics, and more — automatically.
Building upon success

Seamless integration between your CRM and Merchant Service Providers.

The InterWeave Customer Payment Portal provides a secure environment, with seamless integration between your CRM and Merchant Service Providers — allowing real-time ACH/Credit Card payments integrated with your Accounts, Contacts, Opportunities, and Products, and then with your Financial Applications.

The Customer Payment Portal is directly integrated with your CRM Account, Contact, Opportunity, and Product Objects. It's a java application that provides the data structure, workflow, and complex processing logic necessary for fast, reliable, and secure transmission of transaction data.

Once configured, the SmartPortal is available 24/7 for processing transactions — managing routing just like a traditional credit card terminal, but using the Internet.

Secure, Hosted Environment
A fully hosted, PCI-DSS and HIPAA compliant payment environment. Sensitive card data is never stored in your CRM — all processing happens through your configured Merchant Service Provider.
Custom-Branded for Your Business
The SmartPortal can be custom-branded for your business or nonprofit — giving your customers a seamless experience that reflects your brand, not InterWeave's.
Real-time CRM Sync
Every payment creates a Transaction Record in your CRM immediately — linked to the Account, Contact, Opportunity, and Invoice — with no manual data entry by your team.
Financial Application Integration
Payment records flow automatically to QuickBooks, Sage, MS Dynamics, Oracle, and other financial applications — creating Sales Receipts without any manual posting or reconciliation.
Multiple MSPs Simultaneously
Support multiple Merchant Service Providers at the same time — routing different payment types, customers, or business units to different processors based on configurable business rules.
Portal capabilities

A solution that works as hard as you do.

The InterWeave SmartPortal delivers powerful yet easy-to-use configurable integration with your Merchant Service Providers, CRM, and financial applications — with security and reliability built in at every layer.

01
// Online Payment Acceptance
Enables small businesses, nonprofits, and organisations to accept online payments or donations through a secure, hosted portal — with minimal technical setup to link from an existing website.
02
// Multiple Payment Methods
Real-time credit cards, debit cards, mobile wallets, ACH/EFT, and single payments from checking or savings accounts (processed as Web ACH transactions) — all through one configurable portal.
03
// Customer-Selected Amounts
Enhances customer convenience by allowing consumers to select the amount of the transaction — enabling partial payments, overpayments, and donation-style flexible-amount acceptance.
04
// CRM Permissions Hierarchy
Utilises the same authentication and permissions hierarchy as your CRM administration setup — ensuring customers only see their own accounts, invoices, and payment history, with role-based access control.
05
// Invoice Management
Customers review outstanding invoices, due dates, and payment history — all pulled in real time from your CRM. Pay individual invoices, select multiple, or pay the full balance in a single transaction.
06
// Paperless Processing
Streamlines payment processes and reduces paper use — no printed invoices, no mailed cheques, no manual receipt generation. Everything digital, automatic, and reconciled in real time.
