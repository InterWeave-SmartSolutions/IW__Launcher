The SmartSolutions approach

Building upon the success of your financial systems.

InterWeave SmartSolutions builds upon the success of Financial Accounting Applications by delivering powerful yet easy-to-use configurable integration solutions for your CRM. Our SmartSolutions enable seamless uni- or bidirectional integration of data between your Financial Accounting Applications and your CRM.

Complete, pre-built integration solutions between "best of breed" applications. All processes, workflow logic, and mapping are ready for you to configure — using only your mouse and a web interface.

SmartSolutions are fully customizable, allowing you to leverage pick lists, drop-down menus, and custom fields to tailor integrations to your exact needs. Whether you're a start-up, SMB, or enterprise, choose the perfect configuration model for your business — and modify it at any time.

Shared financial processes

QuickBooks, Sage & Dynamics.
More in common than you think.

While they differ in target audience and advanced features, QuickBooks, Sage, and Microsoft Dynamics all share the same core financial processes — and InterWeave integrates all of them through a single, consistent SmartSolutions platform.

The InterWeave advantage

Because all three platforms share the same underlying financial processes, a single InterWeave SmartSolution deployment handles any combination — without rebuilding your integration when you change ERP vendors.
