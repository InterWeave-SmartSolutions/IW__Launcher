Configuration & automation

Deeply configurable. Unique to your business.

Configuring the integration flow from your eCommerce platform to your CRM involves several critical considerations. In-depth configuration options are tailored to your individual business processes and workflows — making your Solution unique to your requirements.

Binding Define how eCommerce customers and orders are matched to existing CRM records — preventing duplicate accounts and contacts through configurable binding criteria.
Contacts Configure primary contact handling — whether the billing contact, shipping contact, or account owner drives the CRM record, with parent-child hierarchy management.
Hierarchy Define parent-child account relationships — mapping eCommerce organizational structures (e.g., corporate accounts with multiple buyers) to your CRM's account hierarchy.
Sync Ops Configure which sync operations run (create, update, delete) per object type — with directional controls for each: eCommerce → CRM, CRM → eCommerce, or bidirectional.
Shipping Map shipping and billing address fields, carrier data, tracking numbers, and fulfilment status between your eCommerce platform and CRM order records.
Custom Map Custom field mapping for any eCommerce platform's proprietary fields — including product attributes, custom order metadata, and store-specific data not covered by standard mappings.
// Automated Processing
InterWeave automates the transfer of eCommerce transactions 7 days a week, 24 hours a day. No manual intervention required — orders flow from store to CRM to financial application automatically.
// Real-time or Batch
Choose real-time sync for instant CRM visibility of new orders, or batch sync at configurable intervals for high-volume stores that process thousands of orders per hour.
// No-Code Configuration
All mapping, binding criteria, hierarchy rules, and sync operations are configured through the InterWeave SmartMap console — no coding, no custom scripts, no developer required.
// Start-up to Enterprise
Whether you're a single-store startup or a multi-brand enterprise, choose the configuration model that fits — and modify it at any time as your eCommerce operation grows and changes.
